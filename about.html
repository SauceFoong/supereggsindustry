About Us

Home » About Us

Our company has been operated since year 2007. Throughout the years, there were many challenges and hardships that encountered by our company. However, our founder insists on keeping his principle which is provide high quality and hygiene products to his customers no matter how bad is the market. It is because our founder believes that being a trustworthy businessman is more important than merely pursuing for profit.

As a food manufacturer, we must ensure that our hygienic condition is meet with the authorities' requirements, and that's the reason why we applied for the Halal certification. By following the SOP which is set by the authorities, we can ensure that our products are safe to consume. Fortunately, our application has been approved successfully and the hygienic condition of our prodcts are able to be maintained throughout the years.

In Super Eggs Trading, we are dedicated to serve our customers with high quality egg-based products. We keep improving ourselves such as increasing our efficiency and also seeking for better business collaboration. For any business enquiry, feel free to contact us by filling the form in the contact page or whatsapp us if you wish to do so.
